DUMBO holds a wide range of land usage, including residential and commercial uses. Furthermore, a 85-acre plot of land by the bridge serves as a park known as Brooklyn Bridge Park. Over time, DUMBO has evolved into a mixed-use community containing warehouses, apartments, loft buildings, restaurants, shops, markets, and offices. Buildings along the Jay Street corridor vary greatly in size, ranging from 1 to 12 stories are likely to be occupied by studios and loft buildings of mixed use. Buildings in the Bridge Street corridor range from 4 to 7 stories, likely serving as office/residential spaces.
