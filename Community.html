DUMBO is a mixed use community, with approximately 63,000 residents and a median age of 35. Compared to neighborhoods in Manhattan, DUMBO holds more attractive rental prices. Since the 1980’s, there has been a steady increase in residential occupancy within loft buildings. The neighborhood’s surplus of loft spaces have attracted creative commercial companies, including design companies, art galleries, and retail shops. According to the 2019 census, there are about 4,200 entrepreneurs, 24,000 workers in private companies, and 3,600 government workers.
